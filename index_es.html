Events & Services

In Bakmie JM we provide various services to our customer for various needs. it can come in the form of the smallest events such as a normal lunch to events that some people can only experience once such as a wedding.

Birthday Party

For both the little ones and the adult ones, Bakmie JM will provide a birthday party like no other. with a special birthday bakmi and enough food for everyone involved.

Wedding Ceremony

Wedding can be a once in a lifetime event for some people, and in bakmi JM we offer you a once in a lifetime experience in your wedding with Bakmi JM, with food performances, and even planning included in the wedding package, so you can enjoy the moment, while we take care of the preparations.

Social Gathering

with Bakmi JM, we believe that you and your friends should be able to connect without the hassle. with our social gathering package we take care of tables, place, time, while you and your friends take care of the gathering.
