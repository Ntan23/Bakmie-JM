About Us

Bakmi JM is a Noodle based restaurant in Indonesia, mainly serving in Jakarta we have the pleassure of serving over 3,000 customer everyday with 20 branches spread accross all of Jakarta

Our history

In the Beginning Bakmi JM was just nothing more than a small noodle cart selling noodles in the streets of Jakarta, born from the minds of 3 freshly graduated College student. Bakmi JM experienced immense growth of customer before openning up their first store in West Jakarta

After some time has passed, Bakmi JM was able to open up their first branch located in South Jakarta with many more openning up to this day, and many more are being planned in the future, to bring the Bakmi JM special to all Indonesians

Our Goal

Our goal from the Beginning of Bakmi JM has been to bring Joy to all Indonesian by using specially served noodles while sticking to the four core tenets of what made Bakmi JM Great, which are :

  • Affordable food for everyone
  • Delicious and delictable noodles
  • Homly experience for everyone
  • Love and Care towards cooking food
